Working Papers

  • Impact of Terrorism on Investment by Firms: Evidence from Jammu & Kashmir, India
    (with Prakarsh Singh)

    Examines how regional conflict and uncertainty shape capital investment and labor spending decisions using firm-level panel data.

  • Guided by Stars: Employee Ratings and the Future of Firm Performance
    (with Prakarsh Singh)

    Studies the relationship between employee sentiment and firm-level financial outcomes.

  • Bank Productivity and Institutional Quality in Nepalese Banking: A DEA-based Assessment
    (M.Sc. thesis with Dr. Rachita Gulati)

    Evaluates the efficiency of 27 Nepalese commercial banks using Data Envelopment Analysis and the Malmquist Productivity Index.
