Research

  • Impact of Terrorism on Investment by Firms: Evidence from Jammu & Kashmir, India. Examined how regional conflict and uncertainty shape capital investment and labor spending decisions using firm-level panel data.

  • Guided by Stars: Employee Ratings and the Future of Firm Performance. Studied employee sentiment and firm-level financial outcomes.

  • Bank Productivity and Institutional Quality in Nepalese Banking: A DEA-based Assessment. Evaluated efficiency of 27 Nepalese banks using DEA and Malmquist Index.
